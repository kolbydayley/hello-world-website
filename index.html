Our Treatment Approach

ERP Therapy

Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP) is the most effective treatment for OCD and anxiety. We help children gradually face their fears in a safe, controlled way while learning that anxiety naturally decreases without performing compulsions.

Our child-friendly approach makes this process manageable and even empowering for young patients.

Family Support

We work closely with parents and family members, providing education about OCD and anxiety while teaching strategies to support their child's recovery at home.

Family involvement is crucial for successful treatment outcomes and long-term maintenance of progress.

Comprehensive Care

Beyond ERP therapy, we provide comprehensive mental health assessments, medication management when appropriate, and coordination with schools and other providers.

Each treatment plan is individualized to meet your child's unique needs and circumstances.
